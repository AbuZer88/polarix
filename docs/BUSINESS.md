# Polarix — Business Model

## Who To Sell To
- Refrigerated transport SMBs (1-20 trucks)
- Food distributors, pharma couriers, flower wholesalers
- Almería region (Spain) + MENA (Arabic markets)
- Pain point: EU ATP compliance + food safety audits

## Pricing
- Starter:  €29/month — 1 sensor, WhatsApp alerts, 30-day log
- Growth:   €79/month — up to 5 sensors, dashboard, PDF reports
- Fleet:    €199/month — unlimited sensors, API access

## Hardware Model
- Client buys sensor (€50 one-time) + monthly plan
- OR Ahmad provides sensor + slightly higher monthly fee
- OR software-only for clients who already have loggers

## MVP Steps
1. Working prototype: 1 sensor + WhatsApp alert (1 weekend)
2. Free pilot: 1 local Almería logistics company (1 week)
3. Fix bugs, validate usefulness (2 weeks)
4. First paying client at €29/month (week 4-5)
5. 10 clients before thinking about scaling

## Strong Sales Hook
EU ATP Regulation requires documented cold-chain proof.
Polarix auto-generates PDF temperature logs for audits.
This alone justifies €29/month to any food transport company.
